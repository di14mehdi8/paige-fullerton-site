Meet Paige

Supported. Understood. Educated.

Hi, I'm Paige — a Realtor with The FI Team proudly serving the greater Spokane area, including Spokane, Spokane Valley, Liberty Lake, North Spokane, South Hill, Mead, Colbert, and surrounding neighborhoods.

The heart behind my business is simple: I want every client to feel supported, understood, and educated through every step of their real estate journey. Whether you're dreaming about your first home, comparing new construction, evaluating investment properties, or exploring duplex and house-hacking strategies to build long-term wealth, I'm here to guide you with clarity, patience, and proven expertise.

With trusted local expertise, deep neighborhood knowledge, strong negotiation skills, and strategic modern marketing, I help you make confident decisions in any market. From pricing strategy and market analysis to negotiation, staging recommendations, and marketing exposure — I bring a thoughtful and data-driven approach to every transaction.

My goal is always to protect your equity, maximize your opportunity, and ensure you feel empowered and informed. I'm committed to being a steady resource, a clear communicator, and a trusted advisor you can rely on.

If you're considering buying or selling in Spokane or simply want to learn more about the current market, new construction options, duplex opportunities, or local neighborhoods — I'm here to help. When you're ready, I'd be honored to help you take your next step.

— Paige
